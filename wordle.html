Wordle

Rules:
Guess the WORDLE in 6 tries

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess wa to the word

Green indicates the letter is in the word and in the correct spot

Yellow indicates the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot

Grey indicates the letter is not in the word or in any spot

Type each guess into your keboard!

Unlike the real WORDLE, there is no dictionary linked, but please only guess real words

Good luck!
